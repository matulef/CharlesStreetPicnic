Frequently Asked Questions

What is this picnic table?
A public art installation inviting anyone to pause, share a meal, or start a conversation—right where you wouldn't expect a picnic table to be.
Do I need to reserve a spot?
Nope. First come, first served. Just be kind and share the space.
How long will it be here?
The table will be in place from June 1 – September 30, 2025 (weather permitting).
Can I bring food?
Please do! Pack your lunch or grab take‑out locally. Just clean up after yourself.
Who built it?
Artist Alex Doe, with reclaimed wood from the old Charles Street bridge.
How can I stay updated?
Leave your email in the contact form and we’ll ping you with any news or events.
