Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Charles Street Picnic?

A picnic table on Charles Street

Why did you make it?

For picnics

Are you sure you didn't make it to cover up an ugly concrete block? It looks like you made it to cover up an ugly concrete block.

Perhaps

Who are you?

A resident of Charles Street who cares

Is this some sort of marketing gimmick?

No

So it's street art, like Banksy?

Yes, like Banksy, exactly

Are you going to get in trouble for it?

Probably

How long will it be here?

Until the concrete block fairies come back to retrieve their lost concrete block

Can I use the plates and silverware?

Yes, but please replace any items you use. The Charles Street Picnic is meant to be shared by the community.

How can I contribute to the Charles Street Picnic?

I am not accepting monetary donations at this time. You are welcome to contribute ideas to picnicmaster@charlesstreetpicnic.com.
